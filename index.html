Capabilities

What Forma Does

01
Intelligent Wall Detection
Computer vision algorithms identify structural walls, partition walls, load-bearing elements, and facade outlines with 99% precision.
02
Parametric Room Analysis
Each enclosed space is automatically classified, labeled, and dimensioned. Room area calculations are generated in real-time.
03
Automated Door & Window Insertion
Openings in walls are recognized and replaced with parametric 3D door and window objects, maintaining original dimensions.
04
Material Intelligence
AI applies appropriate materials based on room type and structural context — concrete walls, glass facades, wooden flooring.
05
Multi-Format Export
Export your 3D model to industry-standard formats including GLB, OBJ, IFC, and FBX for use in any architectural software.
06
Real-Time Collaboration
Share live 3D model links with clients and team members. Annotations, markups, and versioning built into the platform.
